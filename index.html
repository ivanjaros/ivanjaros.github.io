The main service I provide is creativity and direction. My style is unique and differs from most commercial directors, as it should be for everyone. I will never shoot the product or the service directly as the main character, with smiley and happy faces all around it. I do not believe in forcing the subject onto the audience. Instead, I am a firm believer that the audience is smart and wants to consume content that feels more natural, smart and witty. Hence, I will always create a story, symbolism, metaphor or emotion around the product, or service, and make it a part of the the tale we will unveil for the audience. The product, or service, might even become the butt of a joke and the client has to be OK with that in order for me to use my creativity freely, without much restrictions, and produce great content for the audience. I am a strong proponent of contrasts and juxtaposition as well as "show don't tell" mantra, mostly via reaction shots. A simple smile or look says more than a line of dialogue. And that makes the situation much stronger for me than long-winded slogans and lists of attributes and properties of whatever is being promoted.

Please, do not hesitate to contact me with requests for treatments, collaboration, questions, proposals...
